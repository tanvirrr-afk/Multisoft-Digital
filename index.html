Our Process
Four steps. Zero confusion.

We've built a workflow that keeps projects moving and clients informed every step of the way.

01 — Brief

Project Intake

You submit footage, brand guidelines, references, and a deadline. We ask clarifying questions and confirm scope within 24 hours.

02 — Edit

Assembly & Craft

Our editors build the first assembly cut, apply color, add sound design, and deliver a review link — usually within 48 hours.

03 — Review

Feedback Loop

You leave timestamped comments directly on the video. We action all revisions in the next round — two revision rounds included.

04 — Deliver

Final Files

You receive all export formats, project files, and licensed assets. Ready to upload, broadcast, or send to a distributor.
